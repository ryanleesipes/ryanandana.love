Where to Stay

We highly suggest booking your lodging ASAP if you intend to come. The area has a lot of tourism and it can be hard to find a place to last last minute.

Salida

This is Ana's hometown (and where Ana and Ryan are staying) and it offers a wide variety of activities. If you want to extend your stay beyond the day of the party, this town is our recommendation.

Salida Airbnb

Westcliffe

This is a small, rural, mountain town that is closer to the venue. While it doesn't offer as many in-town activities it is still stunningly beautiful and has hiking and other outdoor activities nearby.
